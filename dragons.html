What are God-Dragons?

The God-Dragons are primordial and immensely powerful beings who shaped the Enchanted Realms. Each has two forms, humanoid and dragon, and embodies specific elements and principles through intricate hieroglyphs that govern the world's magic. These God-Dragons bestow magical hieroglyphs and essential knowledge upon ancient human civilization, shaping its development. Together, they created a world where magic and knowledge coexist, guiding and protecting the inhabitants of the Enchanted Realms.

The six God-Dragons are Arion, Aurora, Eldric, Elysia, Finnegan, and Gaia.
